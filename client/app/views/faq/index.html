Where does the candidate information come from?
The candidate information comes from a website called YourNextMP.

Where does the candidates impact and topic data come from?
We use the Majestic API, which takes an MP's website and analyses the backlinks related it to it.

What makes our webiste unbiased?
We have chosen a neutral colour for the website so that we have no affiliation to any party. Also, the candidates are loaded in a completely random order.
